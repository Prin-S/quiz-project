# Coffee Personality Quiz Requirements

## Overview

Build a playful, colorful "What's Your Coffee Personality?" quiz. Visitors answer abstract and quirky questions, then receive coffee recommendations based on the personality types their answers map to.

## Personality and Coffee Pairings

1. **Bold Adventurer** -> Double Espresso
   - Tagline: "You live for intensity"
2. **Sweet Enthusiast** -> Caramel Latte
   - Tagline: "Life's too short for bitter"
3. **Zen Minimalist** -> Black Coffee, Single Origin
   - Tagline: "Simple. Clean. Perfect."
4. **Social Butterfly** -> Cappuccino
   - Tagline: "Coffee is better with company"
5. **Health Nut** -> Oat Milk Americano
   - Tagline: "Wellness in every sip"
6. **Indulgent Treat** -> Mocha with Whip
   - Tagline: "Coffee is dessert"

## Result Logic

- Each quiz answer maps to one personality.
- At the end, tally how many answers mapped to each personality.
- Show all selected personality results as percentages, not just one winner.
- Include the coffee recommendation for each personality shown.
- Example: if a user maps to Bold Adventurer 3 times and Sweet Enthusiast 2 times, show those results as percentages with their recommended coffees.

## Visual Style

Use **Style 1: Playful/colorful**.

Design direction:

- Bright, cheerful colors
- Bold shapes and high-energy layout
- Fun quiz-game feel
- Rounded but not overly soft UI
- Clear progress indicator
- Large, readable question text
- Answer options should feel clickable and lively

## Visual Assets

- Do not use downloaded photo images for the results.
- Create CSS-built coffee illustrations for each result instead.
- Use icons next to answer options to make the quiz feel more polished and visual.

## Quiz Questions

### Question 1

**You find a glowing button labeled "Maybe." What do you do?**

- Press it immediately -> Bold Adventurer
- Ask if it comes with sprinkles -> Sweet Enthusiast
- Sit beside it and observe quietly -> Zen Minimalist
- Invite everyone over to vote -> Social Butterfly
- Check whether it affects sleep quality -> Health Nut
- Save it for a dramatic midnight moment -> Indulgent Treat

### Question 2

**Pick a weather system for your inner world.**

- Thunderstorm over a neon city -> Bold Adventurer
- Cotton-candy sunset -> Sweet Enthusiast
- Clear sky over still water -> Zen Minimalist
- Warm breeze through a street festival -> Social Butterfly
- Fresh mountain air after rain -> Health Nut
- Golden fog around a dessert table -> Indulgent Treat

### Question 3

**A mysterious vending machine offers you one object.**

- A compass that points to risk -> Bold Adventurer
- A tiny cake with your name on it -> Sweet Enthusiast
- A smooth black stone -> Zen Minimalist
- A phone that only calls favorite people -> Social Butterfly
- A reusable bottle that refills itself -> Health Nut
- A velvet ticket to "something lavish" -> Indulgent Treat

### Question 4

**Choose a room to spend an hour in.**

- The room with a secret trapdoor -> Bold Adventurer
- The pink room full of pastries -> Sweet Enthusiast
- The empty white room with perfect silence -> Zen Minimalist
- The room where everyone is laughing -> Social Butterfly
- The sunlit room full of plants -> Health Nut
- The room with candlelight and chocolate -> Indulgent Treat

### Question 5

**Your spirit object is probably:**

- A matchstick -> Bold Adventurer
- A ribbon -> Sweet Enthusiast
- A ceramic bowl -> Zen Minimalist
- A party invitation -> Social Butterfly
- A leaf -> Health Nut
- A gold spoon -> Indulgent Treat

### Question 6

**A tiny oracle gives you advice. What does it say?**

- "Go first." -> Bold Adventurer
- "Make it sweeter." -> Sweet Enthusiast
- "Remove one thing." -> Zen Minimalist
- "Bring someone with you." -> Social Butterfly
- "Drink water too." -> Health Nut
- "Choose the beautiful version." -> Indulgent Treat

### Question 7

**Pick the door you'd open.**

- Red door humming with electricity -> Bold Adventurer
- Candy-striped door with warm light underneath -> Sweet Enthusiast
- Matte black door with no handle -> Zen Minimalist
- Yellow door with music behind it -> Social Butterfly
- Green door covered in vines -> Health Nut
- Burgundy door with a velvet rope -> Indulgent Treat

## Build Notes

- Build this as a real interactive quiz.
- Users should be able to move through questions one at a time.
- Show progress throughout the quiz.
- Let users restart the quiz from the results screen.
- Keep the experience responsive for mobile and desktop.
